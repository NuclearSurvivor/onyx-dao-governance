# PREAMBLE: FOUNDATIONAL DEFINITIONS
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**For purposes of ALL proposals herein:**

### 1. ENTITY DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Community Member"** means any wallet holding XCN tokens on Ethereum, BNB Chain, BASE, or Goliath (upon launch).

**1.2 "Active Community Member"** means a Community Member who has demonstrated verifiable participation within the past 90 days through: governance voting, testnet activity, Zealy engagement (minimum 100 XP), or documented community contributions.

**1.3 "Insider"** means any individual or entity that: (a) receives compensation from the DAO exceeding $10,000 annually, (b) holds a formal DAO role (leadership, ambassador, service provider), or (c) has treasury access or MultiSig authority.

**1.4 "Coordinated Group"** means wallets that have transferred XCN between each other, share common funding sources, or exhibit voting patterns with >95% correlation over 10+ proposals.

**1.5 "Service Provider"** means any entity receiving DAO funds in exchange for services, including but not limited to: Chain, development contractors, marketing firms, auditors, and infrastructure providers.

**1.6 "Leadership"** means individuals holding the following roles: Head of DAO, Head of Business Development, Community Manager, Ambassador Leader, or any role with treasury access or MultiSig authority.

### 2. GOVERNANCE DEFINITIONS

**2.1 "Governance Vote"** means a vote cast through the official Onyx governance mechanism (currently polls.onyx.org and on-chain governance).

**2.2 "Simple Majority"** means approval by more than 50% of votes cast.

**2.3 "Supermajority"** means approval by 60% or more of votes cast, unless otherwise specified.

**2.4 "Strong Supermajority"** means approval by 66.67% (2/3) or more of votes cast.

**2.5 "Constitutional Majority"** means approval by 75% (3/4) or more of votes cast.

**2.6 "Quorum"** means the minimum participation required for a vote to be valid, as defined per proposal type.

**2.7 "Business Day"** means Monday through Friday, excluding internationally recognized holidays.

### 3. FINANCIAL DEFINITIONS

**3.1 "Treasury"** means all XCN and other assets held by DAO-controlled wallets.

**3.2 "Allocation"** means any transfer of treasury funds to external parties.

**3.3 "Grant"** means a one-time allocation for a specific purpose.

**3.4 "Recurring Allocation"** means an ongoing periodic allocation (monthly, quarterly, etc.).

**3.5 "Large Allocation"** means any single allocation exceeding 50,000,000 XCN.

**3.6 "Critical Allocation"** means any single allocation exceeding 500,000,000 XCN.

### 4. PROGRAM DEFINITIONS

**4.1 "Testnet"** means the Goliath testnet environment for pre-mainnet testing.

**4.2 "Mainnet"** means the production Goliath blockchain network.

**4.3 "Testnet Points"** means points earned through testnet participation, calculated at 1 XCN staked/held = 1 Point.

**4.4 "Validator"** means an entity operating node infrastructure to validate transactions on Goliath.

## POLL 1.1: Lower Governance Poll Threshold to 1,000,000 XCN

### Summary
This poll seeks to determine whether the XCN community supports lowering the governance proposal threshold from 10,000,000 XCN to 1,000,000 XCN, enabling broader community participation in DAO governance.

### Current State
The current threshold of 10,000,000 XCN creates a significant barrier to governance participation:

- Total XCN Holders: 60,709
- Wallets with 10,000,000+ XCN: Approximately 100
- Wallets that are burns, protocols, or exchanges: Approximately 50-70
- Actual community members who can create proposals: Approximately 30-50 wallets
- **Percentage of holders excluded from proposal creation: 99.92%**

**Historical Context:**
The original CIP-1, CIP-2, and CIP-3 proposals that established this DAO were passed with only **3-5 voting addresses**. The entire foundation of XCN governance was decided by a handful of wallets.

### Proposed Change
Lower the governance proposal threshold from 10,000,000 XCN to 1,000,000 XCN.

At the 1,000,000 XCN threshold:
- Rank 730 on the holder list holds approximately 1,000,000 XCN
- This expands proposal power from ~50 wallets to ~730 wallets
- Represents a **14x increase** in governance participation capability
- Threshold still significant enough to prevent spam

### Benefits
1. True Decentralization
2. Diverse Perspectives
3. Reduced Insider Control
4. Increased Engagement
5. Alignment with DAO Principles

### Implementation
Single parameter change in governance smart contract. Effective immediately upon passage.

### Vote Options
- **For** — Lower threshold to 1,000,000 XCN
- **Against** — Maintain 10,000,000 XCN threshold
